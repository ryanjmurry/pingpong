Let's Play Some Number Ping Pong!

How this works:

You enter three numbers. The 1st number you give me tells me to generate a list from 1 to your number. From that list, I will change every number in it divisible by your 2nd number to "ping" and every number divisble by your 3rd number to "pong". As a bonus, I will change every number in your list divisible by the product of your 2nd and 3rd number to "pingpong".
